DAMODOT

Adedotun Anthony Adedeji p.k.a. DAMODOT is a Singer | Songwriter, He was born and raised in the vibrant city of Ibadan. Damodot is a very ambitious artist that represents the deep-rooted sounds of afro to the core. It is hard to believe that his journey started recently, creating a sound that leaves his listeners wowed. A track of his "Killaman" is a testament to this as it is his best selling hit till date boasting over 100,000+ streams across all platforms. Despite having embarked on his recording journey merely in 2021, the impact of his work has positioned him as an artist to watch out for in the ever evolving Afrosound Scene.
